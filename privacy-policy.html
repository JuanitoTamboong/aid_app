Privacy Policy

Your privacy and safety are our top priorities

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

AidTracker is designed to help you report emergencies quickly and efficiently while protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Key Principle: No Login Required for Emergency Reporting

You can report emergencies immediately without creating an account or providing personal information. Your ability to get help should never be delayed by registration processes.

Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide emergency services:

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates, approximate location from Wi-Fi/cell towers, or manually entered location
  • Emergency Details: Type of emergency, severity level (optional), number of people involved (optional)
  • Optional Information: Additional notes, photos (if you choose to provide them)
  • Account Information: Only if you choose to create an account (name, phone number, emergency contacts)
  • Technical Data: Device type, app version (for troubleshooting only)

We never collect: Your contacts, messages, call history, browsing history, or any other personal information unrelated to emergency reporting.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Emergency Response: Sharing your location and emergency details with authorized responders
  • Report Updates: Keeping you informed about your report status (if you provide contact info)
  • Service Improvement: Anonymous, aggregated data to improve the app (never tied to you personally)
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or to prevent abuse of emergency services
What We Don't Do

We never sell your data to advertisers. We never use your data for marketing. We never share your data with third parties except emergency responders.

Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary:

  • Anonymous Reports: Automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Account-linked Reports: Retained for 2 years for follow-up and historical reference
  • Photos/Voice Notes: Deleted immediately after emergency response is complete
  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we have about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt out of non-emergency communications
  • Report privacy concerns

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@aidtracker.org

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can see your data
  • Regular Audits: Security audits and penetration testing
  • Employee Training: All staff are trained in data privacy
  • Incident Response: 24/7 monitoring and rapid incident response

Contact Us

For privacy concerns or questions about this policy:

  • Email: privacy@aidtracker.org
  • Mail: AidTracker Privacy Office, 123 Safety Street, City, State 12345
  • Emergency Issues: For immediate concerns about misuse, contact local law enforcement

This policy may be updated periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.
