Privacy Policy

Your privacy and safety are our top priorities

Introduction

AidTracker is designed to help you report emergencies quickly and efficiently while protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Key Principle: No Login Required for Emergency Reporting

You can report emergencies immediately without creating an account or providing personal information. Your ability to get help should never be delayed by registration processes.

Data Collection

We collect only essential information for emergency response:

Location Data
GPS coordinates for accurate emergency response
Emergency Details
Type and description of the emergency situation
Optional Media
Photos of the incident (if you choose to provide them)
Account Information
Only if you choose to create an account for faster reporting

Data Usage

Your data is used solely for:

  • Emergency response coordination - Connecting you with appropriate emergency services
  • Dispatch of appropriate emergency services - Fire, police, medical, or other responders
  • Incident tracking and follow-up - Ensuring proper resolution of emergencies
  • Improving emergency response systems - Enhancing service quality through data analysis
What We Don't Do

We never sell your data, use it for marketing, or share it with unauthorized third parties.

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures:

End-to-End Encryption
All sensitive data is encrypted during transmission and storage
Secure Infrastructure
Protected server infrastructure with advanced security protocols
Regular Security Audits
Continuous monitoring and security assessments
Regulatory Compliance
Adherence to data protection regulations and standards

Data Retention

Emergency reports are retained for:

  • Legal and regulatory compliance - 7 years as required by emergency service regulations
  • Emergency response improvement - Analyzing response effectiveness and efficiency
  • Statistical analysis - Anonymized data used for system enhancement and research
Anonymous reports (without account information) are automatically deleted after 30 days unless required for legal purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access Your Data
Review all personal information we have about you
Request Corrections
Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
Request Deletion
Delete your data (subject to legal requirements)
Report Anonymously
Use the app as a guest without providing personal information
Guest User Protection

You can report emergencies completely anonymously. We do not track or store identifying information for guest users beyond what is necessary for the immediate emergency response.

This policy may be updated periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.
